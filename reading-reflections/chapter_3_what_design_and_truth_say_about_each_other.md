# What Design and Truth Say about Each Other

What Design and Truth Say about Each Other \(p. 27-33\) in _Design and Truth_ by Robert Grudin.

## Prompt

In this chapter, Grudin talks about the transition of design from close dialogue with natural systems to proliferating to the point of being totally separate from those systems. Grudin outlines 13 principles for good design to achieve harmony between nature and technological advancement. Do you agree with him? 

Describe specific examples of designs affirming or negating some of the principles.

## Writing guidelines

1. Length: \(150-300 words\)
2. Reference the chapter content whenever possible
3. Cite sources where applicable.
4. Check for spelling and grammar.
5. Avoid generalizing language. Choose clear and concise wording.
